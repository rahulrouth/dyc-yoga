About Us

Divine Yoga Center started its journey on January 2, 2019. Divine Yoga Center has become one of the most famous names in the field of therapeutic yoga and meditation treatments in Siliguri. At DYC, yoga therapy is performed only by our certified professional yoga instructors and has taught thousands of students and clients with over 4 years of experience and expertise.

We share with you an exclusive studio where you can pursue your inspirational and healthy lifestyles through various forms and styles. We bring and offer you personalized choices to suit your individual health. We offer you the peaceful ancient Indian practice of YOGA, the raw nature of natural fitness and the fluidity and eclecticism of image direction. Yoga and martial arts instructors are all internationally certified and some have over years of teaching experience. They are globally licensed Instructors. Yoga is essentially a spiritual discipline based on an extremely subtle science, focusing on the harmony between mind and body.

At Devine Yoga Center, all of their students and clients pay special attention to their well-being through scientifically controlled yoga practices. There are specially trained instructors with the highest qualifications and skills to guide aspirants.

With years of experience, we'll evaluate you and create a personalized recovery plan that's right for you. We understand how important it is to educate you on the most effective ways to take care of your body so you can heal quickly.
